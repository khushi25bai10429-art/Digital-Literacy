✅ Do’s
Maintain a professional profile picture.
Think before posting content online.
Use privacy settings appropriately.
Verify information before sharing.
Keep your language respectful and professional.
❌ Don’ts
Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly.
Do not post offensive or inappropriate content.
Do not engage in online arguments.
Do not share OTPs, passwords, or financial details.
Do not spread unverified news or rumors.
