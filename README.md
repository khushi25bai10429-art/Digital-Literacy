# Digital-Literacy
# Project Title: Digital Professionalism and Cybersecurity Portfolio

## Overview
This project documents my work across five core modules focused on enhancing digital literacy, professional presence, and cybersecurity awareness. It encompasses the creation of digital presentations, the development of professional portfolios, exploration of digital platforms, mastery of professional email communication, and strategies for cybercrime prevention.


## Module Summaries

### Task 1: Presentation
Focuses on delivering a structured presentation using Canva. 

### Task 2: Portfolio
Documents the setup of my professional digital presence, including profile creation on GiHub, stack overflow LinkedIn and reflections on professional branding.

### Task 3: Platforms
Covers the exploration of HackerRank and evidence of active engagement or testing on these platforms.

### Task 4: Email Etiquette
Contains a collection of drafted emails demonstrating professional tone and structure, along with a personal checklist for effective email communication.

### Task 5: Cybercrime
An analysis of a recent cybercrime case study, including a comprehensive checklist for personal digital safety and threat prevention.
