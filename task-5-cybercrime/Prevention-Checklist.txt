Prevention Checklist

1.  Never share your OTP, passwords, or UPI PIN with anyone.
2.  Remember: Entering your UPI PIN always means you are sending money.
3.  Do not accept unknown UPI collect requests.
4.  Verify phone calls claiming to be from banks or payment apps before
    taking action.
5.  Enable two-factor authentication on all important accounts.
6.  Use strong and unique passwords for different platforms.
7.  Avoid clicking suspicious links in emails, SMS, or WhatsApp
    messages.
8.  Regularly update your banking and payment applications.
9.  Immediately report cyber fraud at the National Cyber Crime Portal:
    https://cybercrime.gov.in
10. Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (24x7) for urgent financial
    fraud reporting.
